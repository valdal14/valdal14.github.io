Description

ChronoTask is a lightweight and intuitive task manager exclusively designed for Apple Watch. Whether you're managing daily to-dos, tracking deadlines, or organizing priorities, ChronoTask ensures you stay on top of your schedule, right from your wrist. Create tasks, set priorities, and assign due dates effortlessly. Get timely local notifications when a tasks is about to expire, and track your productivity with insightful statistic.

Features:

  • Create Tasks and assign priorities and due date.
  • Receive local notifications when a task is about to expire.
  • Dive into detailed statistics to better understand your time management, habits and optimize your to-does and time.
