Description

Looped is a powerful standalone Apple Watch app designed to help you effortlessly track, manage, and stay on top of all your subscription services. With Looped, you can set reminders for upcoming subscription renewals, easily update reminder times, and keep track of how much you're spending over time — all from the convenience of your wrist.

Features:

  • Choose from a list of popular subscription services or add your own custom subscriptions.
  • Set reminders for upcoming subscription renewals and easily update reminder times.
  • Track your spending over time and gain insights into your subscription habits.
  • Receive local notifications when a task is about to expire.
