Description

Frostify is your ultimate food management companion, designed to simplify your life and help reduce food waste. Create virtual fridges and effortlessly keep track of your groceries.

Features:

  • Create virtual Fridges and add food to them.
  • Receive local notifications when items are nearing their expiration date or have expired, helping you stay on top of your food storage.
  • Dive into detailed statistics to better understand your consumption habits and optimize your shopping and storage practices.
